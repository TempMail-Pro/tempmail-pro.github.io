What is Disposable Temporary E-mail?

Disposable email addresses let you receive emails at a temporary address that self-destructs after a set time. They protect your privacy from spam, phishing, and data leaks.

The Tech behind Disposable Email Addresses

These services generate anonymous email accounts using backend APIs like Mail.tm, allowing you to read and manage messages via secure frontend interfaces without needing signup.

Why Would You Need a Fake Email Address?

To register for websites, download resources, or verify accounts without exposing your primary email. Great for temporary tasks, trials, or keeping your inbox clean.

How to Choose a Disposable Email?

Pick a provider with a secure backend, good deliverability, multiple domains, and inbox features like message view, refresh, copy, and delete.

How to Use Disposable Email Address?

Simply copy the generated email, paste it where needed, and check the inbox below for replies or verification codes. Then discard it once done.
